# Definitions/Acronyms
Some of these terms are common industry terms, and some are unique to QubeTeaPi.
- **ConOps** - Concept of operations
- **PCB** - Printed circuit board
- **Pastry**/(Satellite) Bus - The part of the satellite that provides essential functions, separate from **fillings**
- **Fillings**/Payloads - Additional modules added to the **pastry** to perform additional functions and fulfill specific mission requirements
- **SEE** - Single-Event Effect; effects caused when a satellite encounters ionising radiation
- **SEU** - Single-Event Upset; SEEs that cause a state change in a component
- **SEL** - Single-Event Latch-up; SEEs that cause short circuits
