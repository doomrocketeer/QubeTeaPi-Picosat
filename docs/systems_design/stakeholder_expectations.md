# Stakeholder Identification
## By Group
The following groups have been identified as stakeholders:
- **Manufacturers** - companies/individuals that manufacture satellites, who wish to use the QubeTeaPi design standard for professional projects
- **Businesses** - companies/individuals that wish to launch a specific payload into orbit; they will most likely work with manufacturers
- **Universities** - university groups that wish to launch a scientific/experimental payload into orbit
- **Students** - students or student groups/societies that wish to develop a payload and/or use the QubeTeaPi design standard for projects
## By Use Case
The following use cases have been identified:
- People who wish to use existing pastries and fillings for their missions, referred to as **customers**
- People who wish to develop their own fillings for specific missions, referred to as **developers**
- People who wish to expand the QubeTeaPi project by developing new pastries, referred to as **contributors**
It is likely that many stakeholders will fulfill multiple use cases.
# Mission Goals
| ID    | Stakeholder(s)                                                | Need                                                  | Goal                                                                                                       |
| ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| MG-01 | Contributors, Manufacturers, Students                         | To develop a pastry                                   | Provide a comprehensive standard for pastry design                                                         |
| MG-02 | Developers, Manufacturers, Businesses, Universities, Students | To develop a filling                                  | Provide a comprehensive standard for filling design and integration                                        |
| MG-03 | All                                                           | To assemble a QubeTeaPi                               | Design the pastry standard to allow for easy assembly with fillings                                        |
| MG-04 | All                                                           | To integrate fillings into a pastry                   | Establish a standardised bus architecture to connect the pastry and fillings                               |
| MG-05 | All                                                           | To program a QubeTeaPi                                | Provide open-source, modular firmware that shall be adaptable to allow for different pastries and fillings |
| MG-06 | All                                                           | To fly/operate a QubeTeaPi                            | Provide methods for communicating with a QubeTeaPi during a mission                                        |
| MG-07 | Customers, Businesses, Universities, Students                 | To manufacture and use existing pastries and fillings | Provide open-source designs for pastries and some general-use fillings                                     |
